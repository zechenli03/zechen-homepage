About Me

I am a third-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of New South Wales (UNSW), Sydney, supervised by Prof. Flora Salim and Prof. Hao Xue. I am also a Student Researcher at Google.

I am interested in building next-generation health AI systems that connect wearable sensors and large language models. My research spans human activity recognition, continuous glucose monitoring, multimodal health modeling, and LLM4Health, with a focus on interpretable reasoning, personalized insights, and intelligent agents for real-world healthcare applications.
